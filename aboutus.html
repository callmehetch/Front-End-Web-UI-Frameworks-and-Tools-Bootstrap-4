Our Services

The New Dristi Chasma Ghar (Optical Shop), located on the first floor of Dhangadhi, Kailali, Nepal, that offers a versatile selection of eyeglasses, sunglasses and prescription sunglasses. Some of the popular collections include Boss, Kliik, Nicole Miller, Persol, Prada, Ray Ban, and Silhouette.

The optical shop is open to the public Sunday through Saturday and accepts Vision Service Plan (VSP) and Medical Eye Services (MES) insurance. Our outstanding opticians service the public on a walk in basis, no appointment needed.

Hours of operation are Sunday thru Friday 7:30 am – 7:00 pm and Saturday 8:30 am – 2:00 pm with the exception of holidays. For general questions, please call the optical shop directly at (+977) 9848558156.

Please note: the New Dristi Chasma Ghar (Optical Shop) does not issue prescriptions for glasses. If you need a new prescription before purchasing new eyewear, make an appointment with our Optometry Clinic by calling (+977) 9848558156.
